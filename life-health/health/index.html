What is Health Insurance?

Health insurance (also called "Accident and Health" in NJ) covers medical expenses, lost income from disability, and other health-related costs. As a licensed producer, you'll help people find the right coverage to protect them when they get sick or injured.

This section covers everything you need to know for the health portion of the NJ exam - from how policies work to the specific NJ laws you'll be tested on.
