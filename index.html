Quick Reference

ISO (Insurance Services Office)

Creates standardized P&C insurance policies approved by each state

Insurable Interest

Connection to insurer (blood, money, business) - must exist at time of loss

Indemnity Principle

Cannot recover more than the actual financial loss suffered

Law of Large Numbers

More data = better prediction of losses and exposure
