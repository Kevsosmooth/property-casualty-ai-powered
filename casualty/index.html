What is Casualty Insurance?

Casualty insurance protects against legal liability for injuries or damage caused to others. It covers situations where you may be held responsible for:

Bodily Injury

Physical harm to another person

Property Damage

Damage to someone else's property

Personal Injury

Libel, slander, false arrest, etc.
