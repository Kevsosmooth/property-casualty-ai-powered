What is Property Insurance?

Property insurance protects against physical loss or damage to buildings, personal property, and other assets. It covers:

Buildings

Homes, commercial structures, other structures

Personal Property

Contents, belongings, inventory

Loss of Use

Extra expenses, lost income during repairs
